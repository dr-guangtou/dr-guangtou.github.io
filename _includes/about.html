About

My name is Song Huang (黄崧). I am currently a post-doc in astrophysics at IPMU-UTokyo, and working with the astronomers there on the ambitious Hyper-Suprime Camera survey and more broader topics about galaxy evolution. I am from Tianjin, China, and got my Ph.D in Astrophysics from Nanjing University, China.

I am very interested in the formation and evolution of galaxy in general. Particularity, I am really into the life history of the most massive galaxies in the Universe. In our neighborhood, they are mostly quiescent, early-type galaxies. I am trying to study their formation from the angle of their mass-assembly and star-formation history. Other than that, I am also interested in studying the morphology and structure of other types of galaxies.
